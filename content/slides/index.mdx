## Benefits of Git-backed content

<Steps>
  <Step>Ownership over content</Step>
  <Step>Don't need to maintain a database</Step>
  <Step>Works great with open-source</Step>
  <Step>Branches</Step>
  <Step>Easy to work on site locally</Step>
  <Step>Version Controlled!</Step>
</Steps>

## Limitations of Git-backed content

<Steps>
  <Step>Can't handle relational content</Step>
  <Step>Performance on larger datasets</Step>
  <Step>Static content often needs a full-rebuild to apply new changes</Step>
</Steps>
